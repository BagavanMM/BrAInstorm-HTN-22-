Social Media and Mood: Re-imagined

One in ten people struggle with identifying their emotions. This brings a lifetime of frustration, broken relationships, and a lack of meaning. At BrAInstorm, we're reimagining what emotions look like, and how they're interpreted. Using the latest advances in Brain-Computer Interface Technology, BrAInstorm is fostering a new social experience for people suffering from a lack of understanding. We measure waves naturally emitted by the brain during certain states of mood, and using the power of the Stable Diffusion AI model, translate them into tangible manifestations of users' experience.
